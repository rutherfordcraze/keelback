# Ingredients

- 4 pieces salmon
- Lemon juice

# Method

Oven starts at 200Â°C.

1. Put the fish pieces in the centre of a sheet of foil, not touching each other.

2. Squeeze the lemon juice over the fish, and add any flavour ingredients: herbs, garlic, etc.

3. Pull the foil up around the salmon and scrunch the edges together, enclosing it in an airtight pocket.

4. Bake for 20 mins.
