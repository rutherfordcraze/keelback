# Ingredients

- 1 jar korma (or other) paste
- 2 tbsp oil
- Selection of vegetables (e.g. leek, courgette, onion, okra)
- 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped, sliced, or crushed
- 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and diced into 1–2" chunks
- 100ml double cream

# Method

1. Parboil the sweet potato chunks; meanwhile:

2. Heat the oil in a pan and fry up the vegetables and garlic.

3. Add the sweet potato to the vegetables, along with the korma paste and 100ml water. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until the sauce is thickened and the sweet potato is cooked.

4. Add the cream and serve.
