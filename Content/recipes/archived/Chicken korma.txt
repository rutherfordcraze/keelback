# Ingredients

- 1 jar korma paste
- 2 tbsp oil
- Diced chicken
- 50g ground almonds
- 100ml double cream
- (Optional) 25g flaked almonds, toasted

# Method

1. Heat the oil in a pan and cook the chicken pieces until brown all over.

2. Stir in the korma paste, ground almonds, double cream and 100ml water. Simmer for 15 mins until creamy and thickened.

3. (Optional) Serve with toasted flaked almonds sprinkled on top.
