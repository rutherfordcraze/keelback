# Ingredients

- 1 large sweet potato per person
- Diced chorizo
- Feta, cut into cubes
- 2 eggs per person

# Method

1. Peel potatoes and cut into 2cm thick slices.

2. Boil until cooked through, then strain and mash with butter. (This can be done ahead of time if desired.)

3. Fry the chorizo in a frying pan. Once cooked, add the mashed sweet potato and keep cooking until hot and starting to brown in places.

4. When ready, either dish it up or push to one side of the pan. Fry the eggs.

5. Serve all together with the feta cubes on top.
